Once you're signed in, you can test a prompt right away. You have two ways to run:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Try the free grant" icon="gift">
    New accounts get a small, Viorant-funded credit grant so you can test a prompt
    against a managed model **before bringing a key**. It's a trial, not a
    subscription — no card, no markup.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bring your own key" icon="key" href="/providers/byok">
    Add your OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or other provider key in the Model Master.
    Keys live in your OS keychain and never leave your machine.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Run a prompt in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Prompt Master">
    Create a new prompt and write a system instruction — e.g. *"You are a concise
    assistant. Answer in one sentence."*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a model">
    In the Test panel, choose a model. Use the managed trial model, or
    [add your own provider key](/providers/byok) and pick one of yours.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test it">
    Send a message and watch the response stream back live. Switch between
    single-shot and chat to see how the prompt behaves.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  When your trial grant runs out, the managed model greys out with an
  "add your key" prompt. Add a provider key to keep going — that's the BYOK steady
  state.
</Tip>
