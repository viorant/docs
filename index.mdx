The **Viorant Hub** is a local-first desktop app for building AI agents. You author
each part of an agent in a dedicated editor — a **Master** — compose them into a
runnable agent, and run it **on your own machine against your own model keys**.
Nothing is hidden, nothing is brokered: the Hub builds; your agent runs locally.

## Principles you can rely on

- **Local-first.** You author and run on your own machine. Your work doesn't leave it.
- **Bring your own key.** Models run against your provider keys, stored in your OS
  keychain — never on our servers. [Learn more](/providers/byok).
- **Portable & inspectable.** What you build is yours to own, read, and move.
