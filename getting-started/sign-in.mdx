---

The Hub signs you in with **Google**. Sign-in happens in your real browser — never
in an embedded window — so it's the same secure flow you use everywhere else.

## Sign in with Google

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click “Sign in with Google”">
    On first launch, the Hub opens your default browser to Google's sign-in page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your account">
    Pick the Google account you want to use and approve access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to the Hub">
    The browser hands you back to the Hub automatically and you land in the app,
    signed in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If your browser asks whether to open Viorant, allow it — that's the handoff step
  bringing you back into the Hub.
</Note>

## Multiple accounts on one Mac

Each signed-in user gets their own private workspace on the device — your agents,
prompts, and settings are isolated per account. Signing out keeps your data on disk;
signing back in restores it.
